THE GABZ FAV ARTISTS

THE NEIGHBOURHOOD

The Neighbourhood is an alternative rock band with a unique blend of indie pop, R&B, and hip hop influences. Formed in 2011 in Newbury Park, California, the group rose to fame with their debut single "Sweater Weather" in 2012, which became a major hit. Their moody, atmospheric sound, which often incorporates dark, introspective themes, was solidified with the release of their debut album I Love You in 2013.

ARCTIC MONKEYS

Arctic Monkeys are a British indie rock band that helped define the post-punk revival movement. Formed in Sheffield in 2002, they gained widespread attention after self-releasing their demo, and their debut album Whatever People Say I Am, That’s What I’m Not (2006) became an instant success. Known for their energetic sound and witty lyrics, the band's hits like "I Bet You Look Good on the Dancefloor" and "Do I Wanna Know?" made them icons.

THE XX

The xx is a London-based band formed in 2005, known for their minimalist approach to indie pop and dream pop. Their music features soft, haunting vocals paired with electronic beats, creating a subdued yet captivating atmosphere. They garnered critical acclaim with their 2009 debut album xx, which won the prestigious Mercury Prize and introduced fans to hits like "Intro" and "Crystalised."

LATIN MAFIA

Latin Mafia is a rising group in the Latin urban music scene, blending reggaeton and Latin trap with modern urban beats. Representing Latin strseet culture, their sound is gritty and raw, with lyrics that reflect life in the streets. Although newer to the scene, they have quickly made a name for themselves by mixing classic reggaeton rhythms with a modern, trap-influenced edge, attracting fans from across the Latin music spectrum.
